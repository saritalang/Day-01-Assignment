Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients:

  • Spaghetti
  • Bacon
  • Eggs
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Black Pepper

Preparation Steps:

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. Fry bacon until crispy and set aside.
  3. Mix eggs and Parmesan in a bowl.
  4. Combine cooked spaghetti, egg mixture, and bacon.
  5. Serve with black pepper on top.
